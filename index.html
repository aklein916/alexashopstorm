    Customer Support

    Exceptional Customer Support does not always involve saying "yes". However, a direct "no" is not effective. Communication is critical: When I listen to a customer's concerns I can understand the root of the problem and find a solution that makes the customer happy and does not undermine the company's best practices. Because while the customer is not always right, the customer is never wrong, either. The solution to any problem starts with listening and managing expectations.
